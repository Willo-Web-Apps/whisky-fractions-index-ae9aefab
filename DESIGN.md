# Design DNA — Vatted

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: product-led
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary: #8B4513 → oklch(0.42 0.12 45) — dark amber / saddle brown
- Accent: #C8973A → oklch(0.67 0.12 72) — warm gold
- Surface Dark: oklch(0.10 0.025 45) — near-black with amber undertone
- Surface Mid: oklch(0.14 0.025 45) — deep oak
- Text: oklch(0.93 0.04 82) — warm cream

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 300–700 (luxury editorial, elegiac serifs for headlines)
- Body: Montserrat — weights 300–600 (crisp, global, financial clarity)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Dark amber editorial theme**: The hero and footer live in near-black amber space with layered radial/conic gradients evoking candlelight — not crypto neon. A noise texture overlay adds tactile, material depth.
2. **Gold typographic hierarchy**: Key metrics, numbers, and accent phrases rendered in warm gold (#C8973A) against dark surfaces — like ledger gold lettering in a private spirits trading room.
3. **Asymmetric split feature sections**: Deep-dive features alternate left/right with a thin gold vertical rule divider and editorial serif headlines contrasting crisp Montserrat data labels — Bloomberg terminal meets master distiller's library.
